Chatake Greenworks
India's Biogas Opportunity
India's SATAT initiative targets 5,000 compressed biogas plants generating 15 MT of CBG annually. GFIS is the intelligence layer.
Feedstock Intelligence AI-optimized biomass mix from agricultural waste, MSW, and livestock dung for maximum CH₄ yield.
Carbon Credit Monetization Automated ESG reporting and verified carbon offset calculations aligned with VCS/Gold Standard protocols.
ATEX-Grade IoT Sensors Explosion-proof sensors for hazardous environments — real-time CH₄, CO₂, H₂S telemetry.
Research & Training
Innovation Ecosystem
Full technical architecture — from IoT edge nodes at biogas digesters to cloud MLOps pipelines and ESG reporting APIs.
XGBoost + LSTM Ensemble Hybrid model combining gradient boosting for tabular sensor data with LSTM for time-series biogas yield forecasting.
Maharashtra Spatial Database GIS-indexed database of 5000+ plant locations, feedstock zones, and grid connectivity for route optimization.
