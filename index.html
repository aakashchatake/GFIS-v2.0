India's Biogas
Opportunity
India's SATAT initiative targets 5,000 compressed biogas plants generating 15 million metric tonnes of CBG annually. GFIS is the intelligence layer that makes this scale possible — from feedstock optimization to carbon credit monetization.
🌾
Feedstock Intelligence AI-optimized biomass mix from agricultural waste, municipal solid waste, and livestock dung for maximum CH₄ yield.
💰
Carbon Credit Monetization Automated ESG reporting and verified carbon offset calculations aligned with international VCS/Gold Standard protocols.
📡
ATEX-Grade IoT Sensors Explosion-proof sensors for hazardous biogas environments — real-time CH₄, CO₂, H₂S, temperature, and pressure telemetry.
